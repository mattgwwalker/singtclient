Virtual Choir Client: Mockup Interface

This is version 0.1 of software that allows participation in a Virtual Choir rehearsal. It will allow you to connect to a rehearsal space and participate in a joint rehearsal. You will be able to hear others, talk to them, and sing together. The conductor will be able to hear everyone as they sing (synchronised and in real-time) and you will be able to hear everyone after you've finished each recording.

Setup

This software does not have echo cancelling. As a consequence, it's very important that your microphone cannot hear your speakers. The easiest solution is to wear headphones when connected to a rehearsal space.

Setting the Microphone's Level

The first step is to ensure that we have set an appropriate level for your microphone. You will be recorded for a few seconds and during that time you need to sing loudly. If you adjust your microphone, or you adjust your position relative to your microphone, then you may have to repeat this process.

Press the button "Set Gain for Singing". Sing loudly.

A peak of -23dB was detected, that's not too bad. You might want to consider adjusting things so that you're closer to the microphone and then repeat the process.

Connecting

After setting the microphone's level, you will want to connect to the rehearsal space. The connection details were shared with you by email. Enter them in the box below.

Next, enter your name in the box below. This will be displayed to those in the rehearsal space.

When you're ready, press the "Connect" button below. You will then join the rehearsal and be able to hear and speak with the others in the choir.

Muting

It's become good etiquette to mute your microphone when you're in an online conference and you're not speaking. To mute your microphone when you're connected, press the "Mute Microphone" button below. You will need to unmute your microphone to speak and to sing.

If your microphone is muted when the others are singing, you will be able to hear them in real-time. However, if you mute your microphone during a recording you will not be able to re-join the recording even if you unmute the microphone.

Monitoring

During rehearsal, the conductor will instigate recordings. During recordings, if your microphone is live and unmuted, you will hear a backing track and you may also monitor your own recording. (You will not be able to hear the others singing in real time.)

Leaving

If you wish to leave the rehearsal, you may click on the button "Leave Rehearsal". You may reconnect after leaving if the rehearsal is still ongoing.
